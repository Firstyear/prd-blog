USG fixing avahi

Sadly on the USG pro 4 avahi will regularly spiral out of control taking up 100% cpu. To fix this, we set an hourly restart:

sudo -s
crontab -e

Then add:

15 * * * * /usr/sbin/service avahi-daemon restart
